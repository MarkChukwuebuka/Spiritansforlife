who are seminarians for life?

“Students for life” is a pro-life movement, and an association of seminarians (young men seeking to be future Roman Catholic priests) who have chosen to live chastely and to promote, protect, defend and respect the dignity and sanctity of human life from conception to natural death, in other words, this movement challenges seminarians and members of related movements in other institutions to chastity, urges them to promulgate the awareness of pro-life values and virtue in their fellow seminarians/students, and spurs them on to promote, defend, protect and respect human life.
